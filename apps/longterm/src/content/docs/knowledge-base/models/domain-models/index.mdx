Domain models focus on AI risks within specific application areas like bioweapons, cyberweapons, and disinformation. They provide domain-specific analysis of how AI capabilities interact with existing threats.

## Models in This Category

- **Bioweapons AI Uplift**: How AI might enhance bioweapons development capabilities
- **Bioweapons Attack Chain**: Modeling the bioweapons development and deployment chain
- **Autonomous Weapons Escalation**: Escalation dynamics in autonomous weapon systems
- **Autonomous Weapons Proliferation**: Modeling spread of autonomous weapons technology
- **Cyberweapons Attack Automation**: How AI enables automated cyber attacks
- **Cyberweapons Offense-Defense**: Offense-defense balance in AI-enabled cyber operations
- **Deepfakes Authentication Crisis**: How deepfakes undermine content authentication
- **Disinformation Detection Race**: Arms race between generation and detection of disinformation
- **Fraud Sophistication Curve**: Trajectory of AI-enabled fraud capabilities
