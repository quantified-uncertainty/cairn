Governance models analyze how institutions, governments, and other actors can govern AI development. They examine coordination challenges, public opinion dynamics, and institutional capacity.

## Models in This Category

- **International Coordination Game**: Game-theoretic analysis of international AI governance
- **Institutional Adaptation Speed**: How fast institutions can adapt to AI changes
- **Multi-Actor Landscape**: Mapping key actors and their relationships in AI governance
- **Whistleblower Dynamics**: Dynamics affecting disclosure of AI safety concerns
- **Public Opinion Evolution**: How public attitudes toward AI evolve over time
- **Media-Policy Feedback Loop**: Interaction between media coverage and policy responses
