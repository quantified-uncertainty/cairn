Analysis models provide meta-level frameworks for understanding the overall AI risk landscape, critical uncertainties, and technical pathways. They help structure high-level strategic thinking.

## Models in This Category

- **AI Risk Portfolio Analysis**: Portfolio approach to analyzing different AI risk scenarios
- **Compounding Risks Analysis**: How multiple AI risks compound and interact
- **Critical Uncertainties**: Key uncertainties that most affect AI risk estimates
- **Technical Pathways**: Technical development pathways and their implications
- **Proliferation Risk Model**: Modeling the spread of dangerous AI capabilities
- **Warning Signs Model**: Identifying early warning indicators for AI risks
