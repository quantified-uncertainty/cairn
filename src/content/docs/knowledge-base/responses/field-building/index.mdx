## Overview

Field-building focuses on **growing the AI safety ecosystem** rather than doing direct research or policy work. The theory is that by increasing the number and quality of people working on AI safety, we multiply the impact of all other interventions.

| Approach | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **[Field Building & Community](/knowledge-base/responses/field-building/field-building/)** | Training programs, community, funding, public awareness |

---

## Key Activities

### Training & Education
- ARENA, MATS, AGI Safety Fundamentals
- University programs and courses
- Research mentorship

### Community Building
- Conferences and gatherings
- Online forums (Alignment Forum, LessWrong)
- Career support and mentorship

### Funding
- Open Philanthropy, SFF, EA Funds
- Individual donors
- Government funding (emerging)

### Public Awareness
- Books, podcasts, videos
- Expert statements and testimony
- Media engagement

---

## Theory of Change

Field-building is a **meta-level intervention**:
- More capable people → More research + policy progress
- Better infrastructure → More effective work
- Larger community → More diverse perspectives and approaches
