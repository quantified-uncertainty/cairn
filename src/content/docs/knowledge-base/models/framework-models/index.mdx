Framework models provide general-purpose analytical structures for thinking about AI risk. They offer conceptual foundations that inform more specific analyses.

## Models in This Category

- **Carlsmith Six Premises**: Joe Carlsmith's framework decomposing existential AI risk into six key premises
- **Instrumental Convergence Framework**: Analysis of convergent instrumental goals in advanced AI
- **Defense in Depth Model**: Layered safety approach for AI systems
- **Capability Threshold Model**: Framework for when specific capabilities become dangerous
