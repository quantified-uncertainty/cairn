Societal models analyze how AI technologies affect social structures, human behavior, and societal dynamics. They examine effects on trust, autonomy, power structures, and collective behavior.

## Models in This Category

- **Authoritarian Tools Diffusion**: How AI surveillance tools spread to authoritarian regimes
- **Consensus Manufacturing Dynamics**: How AI enables manufacturing of false consensus
- **Expertise Atrophy Progression**: Progression of human skill atrophy from AI reliance
- **Lock-in Mechanisms**: Mechanisms by which AI might lock in suboptimal outcomes
- **Post-Incident Recovery**: Recovery dynamics following major AI incidents
- **Preference Manipulation Drift**: How AI-driven preference manipulation accumulates
- **Reality Fragmentation Network**: How AI contributes to fragmented information environments
- **Societal Response**: Modeling societal responses to AI-induced changes
- **Surveillance Authoritarian Stability**: How AI surveillance affects authoritarian stability
- **Sycophancy Feedback Loop**: Dynamics of AI sycophancy and its effects
- **Trust Erosion Dynamics**: Dynamics of trust erosion from AI-generated content
