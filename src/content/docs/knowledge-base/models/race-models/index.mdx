Race models analyze competitive dynamics where multiple actors are racing toward AI capabilities. They examine how competition affects safety investments and outcomes.

## Models in This Category

- **Capability-Alignment Race**: Race between capability advances and alignment progress
- **Winner-Take-All Concentration**: Dynamics of market concentration in AI development
