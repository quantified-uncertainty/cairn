Cascade models analyze how initial failures or changes propagate through interconnected systems, potentially amplifying into larger-scale impacts. They help identify intervention points before cascades become uncontrollable.

## Models in This Category

- **Risk Cascade Pathways**: How initial AI failures cascade into larger systemic risks
- **Trust Cascade Model**: Propagation of trust erosion through institutions
- **Expertise Atrophy Cascade**: How human skill degradation compounds over time
- **Cyber Psychosis Cascade**: Progression of reality-detachment through AI-mediated experiences
- **Automation Bias Cascade**: How over-reliance on AI compounds through systems
