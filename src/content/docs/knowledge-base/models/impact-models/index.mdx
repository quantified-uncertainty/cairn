Impact models estimate the magnitude, scope, and distribution of potential AI-related impacts on society. They help quantify the stakes and prioritize interventions.

## Models in This Category

- **Economic Disruption Impact**: Estimating economic impacts from AI-driven disruption
- **Disinformation Electoral Impact**: How AI-generated disinformation might affect elections
- **Surveillance Chilling Effects**: Impact of AI surveillance on behavior and expression
