Intervention models help evaluate the effectiveness and priority of different safety interventions. They inform resource allocation and strategic planning for AI safety efforts.

## Models in This Category

- **Intervention Effectiveness Matrix**: Comparing effectiveness of different safety interventions
- **Safety Research Allocation**: Optimal allocation of safety research resources
- **Safety Research Value**: Estimating the value of marginal safety research
- **Worldview Intervention Mapping**: Mapping interventions to underlying worldviews about AI risk
